Apply for Incubation

TBI Incubates early-stage Med-tech devices, which provides healthcare solutions to the poor. We handhold start-ups that are have built a minimum viable product that has early traction and a committed team working full time on creating an impactful solution for the poor

What we look for?

Innovation

Inovation in creating sustainable solutions in the Healthcare domain.

Affordable

Affordable solutions for marginal communities in India leading to a social impact.

Sustainable

Sustainable for-profit start-ups which have a viable business model in the long run

Scale

Enterprises which can scale their solutions across geographies thus multiplying the impact.

The Process

Application Submission

Ensure you give maximum details while submitting your application form. It will assist us in evaluating your company and its solution better

Preliminary Assessment

Our Incubation team will connect with those enterprises that are in alignment with our mandate and assess the details shared by you

Due Diligence

After mutual agreement on incubation interest and selection, our team will dive into in-details, visit the sites, or interact with the stakeholders to understand your enterprise & its impact better

Investment Committee

Finally, you will pitch to a group of experts that will evaluate your application for further process and investment decisions.
